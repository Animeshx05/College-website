Kazi Nazrul University (KNU), anascent university in the district of Paschim Bardhaman of West Bengal, runs on its campus and also in its affiliated colleges Diploma, Under Graduate, Post Graduate, M Phil and Ph.D courses in the Faculty of Arts and fine Arts, Faculty of Science & Technology, Faculty of Law and Faculty of Commerce & Management. It caters to the need of more than forty four thousand students every year.

The university has a team of highly qualified and experienced faculty members who are committed to providing quality education to the students. The university also encourages research activities and provides opportunities for students and faculty members to participate in research projects.

Apart from academics, Kazi Nazrul University also focuses on extracurricular activities and sports. The university has various clubs and societies for students interested in music, dance, drama, and other cultural activities. The university also has a well-equipped sports complex for students interested in sports and athletics.

Overall, Kazi Nazrul University is a good choice for students who are looking for quality education and a well-rounded university experience in Asansol, West Bengal.
