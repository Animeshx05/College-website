From the Principal’s Desk

Dr. G. M. Helaluddin
Principal

Welcome to Michael Madhusudan Memorial College situated at a very prime location of the vibrant city of Durgapur. To fulfill the growing need for education and to develop human resources in the locality, the college was established in the year 1996. Our College’s aim is to educate the youth of the state to take their productive place as leaders in the Indian community by offering our pupils a comprehensive undergraduate education. We are a caring community where students’ needs are a priority and human values are respected and encouraged to coexist. The curriculum emphasizes the acquisition of knowledge, useful skills, understanding, critical thinking, and problem-solving abilities for all students attending our College. Management and staff believe that every student is unique and special and that they all have the ability to learn. We believe it is our responsibility as educators to nurture and develop every student to his/her maximum potential.
